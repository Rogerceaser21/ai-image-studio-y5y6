RACE Recap · Now for Images

The RACE Framework

R · Role
Who should the A.I. pretend to be? (e.g. children's book illustrator, anime artist)
A · Action
What should the A.I. draw or generate? (e.g. draw a sea creature)
C · Context
What is the scene or story? (e.g. coral reef, big eyes, fish approaching)
E · Expectation
What should it LOOK like? (style, colour, mood — e.g. bright watercolour)

You Do

Your Turn

Step 1 · 3 min

PLAN on paper

Write your R / A / C / E down BEFORE you type. Each letter must say something.

Step 2 · 5 min

GENERATE in Gemini

Paste your RACE prompt into the A.I. image tool. See what comes out.

Step 3 · 4 min

REFINE and re-run

Change ONE letter of RACE (usually C or E). Generate again. Compare.

Safety · Read this

  • No real people (no classmates, teachers, family, celebrities).
  • No personal info in your prompt (name, address, school).
  • School-appropriate only. Animals, characters, scenes, vehicles, food, fantasy.
  • If the A.I. makes something strange or upsetting: hand up immediately. Don't show others.
